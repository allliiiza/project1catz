Kneading or “making biscuits” is a sign of contentment and happiness. Cats knead their mothers when they are nursing to stimulate the let-down of milk.

When a cat flops over and exposes their belly, it's to show they're relaxed and showing trust.

When your cat sticks his butt in your face, he is doing so as a gesture of friendship.
